ABOUT US

Zenin (Maypole Pvt. Limited) is a prestigious fashion clothing brand established in 2017 by Warda Sohail and Vasam Sohail. Driven by a vision to create captivating and comfortable outfits for women of all ages and styles, Zenin is dedicated to offering its customers high-quality products at accessible prices. A dedicated team of highly skilled professionals, including design and textile experts, marketing strategists, and master craftsmen, collaborates to bring you the most appealing and trending outfits. Whether it’s stitched or unstitched, western or eastern, formal or semi-formal, Zenin excels in every category, including fragrances, body mists, accessories, bags, and footwear. Our clothes are crafted with vibrant color palettes, exquisite embroideries, delicate embellishments, smooth fabrics, and flawless prints. We design with a blend of cultural heritage and contemporary styles, creating outfits that epitomize sophistication and elegance. Our collections feature bold, playful, and avant-garde ensembles, always keeping the customer’s preferences in mind. At Zenin, top management ensures a well-organized workspace, effective policies, strict discipline in operations, and high manufacturing standards. Our in-house production covers product design, fabric selection, inspection, pattern making, crafting, and sewing. With over 80 outlets in prime locations and malls across Pakistan, our stores offer a superb shopping experience with well-equipped, aesthetically pleasing spaces, professional staff, excellent service, and convenient product displays. Choose the finest. Choose Zenin!
